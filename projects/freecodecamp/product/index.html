Passione - the best Mafia gang in Italy

Powerful Organization

Passione is one of the most powerful gangs in Italy. The gang is based in Naples, but operatives from Rome and Venice are not unheard of.

Talented Members

Passione possesses a high number of Stand users, due to its harsh recruiting test. In order to join, one must be approved by a lieutenant in the gang through an interview. For details please consult Polpo.

High Income

Passione controlled Naples' hotels, port construction sites, and restaurants. Members earn a good salary.
